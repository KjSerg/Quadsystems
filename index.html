About
Who we are

QUAD (Quantum Analytical Detection) Systems was founded in 2020 by Dr. Klemens Kessler with a vision to advance NMR spectroscopy. Based in Zurich, we design, manufacture and integrate cutting-edge NMR spectrometer components for academic and industrial use.

Our global team combines decades of expertise in high-field NMR technology. We work with trusted partners across Europe and North America to deliver precise, innovative solutions tailored to modern research needs.
