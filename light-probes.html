Home / Products / Probes / Light probes

Light probes

High-performance liquid-state NMR probe with competitive sensitivity and line shapes from 300 to 600 MHz with lock, actively shielded z-gradient with fast recovery and compatible with QUAD, Bruker or Varian spectrometers. Works as a regular probe when lights are off.

Integrated LED illumination system

  • LEDs integrated inside gradient
  • Several wavelengths available
  • TTL-triggered
  • Fully programmable light sequences (QUAD, Bruker, Varian)
  • No fibers, no tube inserts
  • Compatible with full automation
Key advantages
High performance (300–600 MHz)

Reliable sensitivity and sharp line shapes with full lock capability.

Integrated LED illumination

LEDs built into the gradient — no fibers or tube inserts required.

Automation & Compatibility

Works with QUAD, Bruker, Varian. TTL-triggered, fully programmable.

Standard or LED mode

Functions as a regular NMR probe when LEDs are turned off.
