Advancing NMR spectroscopy with precision & innovation

Advancing NMR spectroscopy with precision & innovation Founded in Zurich, QUAD Systems is a global innovator in high-performance NMR spectrometer components — powered by decades of experience and visionary leadership.

Who we are

QUAD (Quantum Analytical Detection) Systems was founded in 2020 by Dr. Klemens Kessler with a vision to advance NMR spectroscopy. Based in Zurich, we design, manufacture and integrate cutting-edge NMR spectrometer components for academic and industrial use.

Our global team combines decades of expertise in high-field NMR technology. We work with trusted partners across Europe and North America to deliver precise, innovative solutions tailored to modern research needs.

We specialize in advancing the capabilities of nuclear magnetic resonance (NMR) systems, striving for excellence in every aspect of design, integration, and performance. Our solutions not only enhance research capabilities but also streamline processes, making complex analysis more efficient and accessible. By collaborating with leading academic institutions and industries, we ensure our technologies remain at the forefront of scientific innovation, providing essential tools for researchers and scientists worldwide.

Our mission

To redefine the boundaries of what’s possible in NMR spectroscopy by delivering modular, upgradeable, high-performance solutions accessible to scientists worldwide.  We aim to simplify complex workflows and accelerate discovery through cutting-edge engineering.
