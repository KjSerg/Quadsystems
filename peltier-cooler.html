Peltier cooler

Advanced shim systems designed for seamless integration with new or existing magnets, enabling high field homogeneity and NMR system upgrades.

Effortless low-temperature operation

Our sample changers and automation systems can be configured to individual customer needs and magnet types. Coupled to our patented tuning and matching technology they offer the greatest efficiency in NMR studies.

  • Convenient and efficient
  • For measurement down to 5°C
  • No air compressor, no vibrations

Optionally: Heat exchanger with liquid nitrogen for low-temperature measurements

Key advantages
Vibration-free cooling

Solid-state cooling without air compressors ensures silent and stable operation.

Down to 5°C

Supports precise temperature regulation down to 5°C — ideal for biochemical and material studies.

Nitrogen heat exchanger option

Enables even lower temperature capabilities using liquid nitrogen extension.

Plug & Play integration

Fully compatible with QUAD and OEM NMR setups — easy to install and operate.
