Sample changers

Advanced shim systems designed for seamless integration with new or existing magnets, enabling high field homogeneity and NMR system upgrades.

Customizable sample changers for any magnet setup

Our sample changers and automation systems can be configured to individual customer needs and magnet types. Coupled to our patented tuning and matching technology they offer the greatest efficiency in NMR studies.

Key advantages
Flexible configuration

Adaptable to various magnet types and lab requirements with customizable settings.

Automation-ready

Seamlessly integrates with automated NMR workflows to reduce manual handling.

High efficiency

Boosts throughput in NMR studies thanks to precise tuning and matching technology.

System compatibility

Compatible with QUAD, Bruker, and Varian systems for maximum lab flexibility.

Automate sample cycling to increase efficiency in NMR experiments with large sample volumes or repetitive studies.
