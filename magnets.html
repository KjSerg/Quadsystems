Magnets

QUAD’s magnets are designed in collaboration with our global network of specialist partners.

Performance overview

QUAD Systems AG designs high-field NMR magnets for long-term research use, ensuring field stability, low drift, and minimal helium loss. Below are the core performance specifications:

  • Amplitude/phase/frequency resolution:12/16/32 bit
  • Timing resolution/ minimum duration:12.8 ns / 115.2 ns
  • Spectral width:up to 4.88 MHz
  • Bandwidth:2.4 MHz
  • Gradient amplifier:10 A
  • Gradient amplifier:10 A
  • Gradient amplifier:10 A
Key advantages
Low helium consumption

Helium boil-off under 120 l/year helps reduce maintenance and operational costs in long-term NMR use.

Exceptional field stability

< 0.01 ppm/hour drift ensures stable, high-precision results even in long-duration experiments.

Compact, actively shielded design

Actively shielded magnets simplify installation and minimize stray fields in lab environments.

System compatibility

Fully compatible with QUAD probes and consoles. Easily integrates into Bruker or Varian setups.
