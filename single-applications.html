Automated High-Throughput Screening

Unlock faster, smarter screening with advanced automation tailored for modern NMR workflows.

  • Sample Changer + System Integration
  • Chemical
Overview

Automated high-throughput screening (HTS) is a cornerstone technique in modern NMR laboratories where large-scale, repetitive sample analysis is required. By integrating intelligent sample changers with robust NMR spectrometers, users can dramatically improve processing speed, consistency, and resource efficiency.

This setup is especially beneficial for:

  • Pharmaceutical screening of compound libraries
  • Longitudinal biological studies
  • Food authentication and quality control
  • Industrial material testing
Introduction

In modern NMR laboratories, time and accuracy are critical. Automated High-Throughput Screening transforms sample processing by allowing researchers to analyze dozens—or even hundreds—of samples in a continuous, unattended workflow. This solution not only increases daily throughput but also significantly reduces human error, ensuring consistent, high-quality data every time.

  1. Sample Preparation: Users load samples into standard or customized sample holders compatible with the NMR system.
  2. Rack Integration: The changer reads position IDs and queues samples for analysis.
  3. Temperature & Lock Adjustment: Optional pre-scan conditions such as thermal equilibrium or locking are applied automatically.
  4. NMR Data Acquisition: The system executes preconfigured protocols (1D/2D scans, delay loops, etc.) for each sample.

How the System Works

At the heart of the system is a programmable sample changer integrated with our patented fast tuning and matching technology. Once the samples are loaded and the acquisition sequence is defined, the system takes full control—loading each sample, executing the experiment, and storing the data. Advanced scheduling allows for customizable measurement conditions and parallel optimization across different samples.

Practical Applications

This technology is ideal for laboratories that process large batches of samples on a daily or weekly basis. Whether you're screening new drug compounds, analyzing polymers, or conducting long-term material testing, the automated workflow ensures reliability, repeatability, and speed. It's a game-changer for sectors like life sciences, food chemistry, environmental analysis, and materials research.

System Integration

Our sample changers can be configured for compatibility with most OEM magnets, including Bruker and Varian systems. Integration is seamless and non-invasive, preserving the integrity of your existing setup while enhancing its capabilities. Remote monitoring and smart diagnostics provide added convenience for modern lab environments.

Key Benefits

Manual sample handling in NMR is time-consuming and prone to variability. With our automated screening solutions, labs can streamline their workflow, reduce operator fatigue, and maintain uniform measurement conditions. Automation enables 24/7 operation, which is particularly valuable for high-demand environments such as pharmaceutical R&D, chemical synthesis monitoring, and quality control processes.

  • Increased Productivity: Run more samples in less time without increasing staff workload.
  • Improved Data Consistency: Automated operation minimizes human-induced variability.
  • Flexibility: Easily configurable to different experiment types and sample formats.
  • Scalability: From small labs to industrial-level throughput, the system grows with your needs.
“Thanks to the automated sample changer, we were able to triple our screening capacity while significantly reducing hands-on time and variability. The system now runs day and night with minimal supervision, delivering consistent, high-quality results.” — Dr. Lena Hoffmann, Lead Scientist, Molecular Screening Lab.
